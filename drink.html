Mini Vending Machine

Drink

Drink is a miniature vending machine concept that turns the familiar language of Japanese street convenience into a cinematic ambient object. Its glowing buttons, softly lit product window, and nostalgic proportions create a desk piece that feels both playful and unexpectedly meditative.

Through a concealed touch control, the object cycles through environmental lighting modes such as Rain, Dusk, Winter Night, and Warm Glow. Each mode changes the color temperature and atmospheric character of the piece, allowing the user to shift the emotional tone of the desk with a single gesture.

The gradual transitions are designed to mimic the physical rhythm of real illuminated machines, making each change feel like a small scene transformation rather than a simple interface response. The result is a collectible object rooted in memory, mood, and urban intimacy.

  • Material direction: Compact molded body, illuminated display windows, tactile controls
  • Display idea: Ambient mode switching, time, stylized product interface
  • Mood: Tokyo rain, nostalgic glow, urban night calm
