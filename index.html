Design Direction

Three qualities that define the collection

Emotional Utility

These objects frame time, mood, status, transit memory, or imagined places in ways that gently support daily life.

Miniature Architecture

Windows, shelters, station signs, and small public structures are reinterpreted as desk-scale collectible forms.

Cinematic Warmth

Warm light, restrained materials, and atmospheric composition give each piece a calm, restorative quality.

About the Studio

Between magical realism, urban memory, and tabletop ritual.

Concept Theory explores how everyday objects can hold narrative, softness, and spatial imagination. The work draws from storybook architecture, Japanese signage, miniature transit systems, and intimate domestic light.
